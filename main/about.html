Dungeon Hop - HOW TO & CREDITS

HOW TO

1

START THE GAME

Enter your preferred IP Adress: If you like to play on the foreseen server pleas enter 'os'.

Enter your Name.

2

WHO WINS?

Who can survive in this evil dungeon world without falling into lava or get hit by a monster.

Who's not too slow to fall off the world (get hit by browser borders).

3

HOW TO PLAY AGAIN?

Follow the instructions: Just be patient until the game reloads.

Then press start. When everybody's ready you can start a new adventure.

4

HOW MANY PLAYERS CAN PLAY IN ONE GAME?

Currently you can play alone or with up to four people.

Your opponents will be added to the game randomly.
